# Phase 3: Trust, Credibility & Lead Generation

## Goal

**As a** visitor to the Seacoast Construction website, **I want to** see proof of credibility (certifications, portfolio, testimonials) and easily request a consultation, **so that** I trust the company enough to submit my project details.

## must_haves

- [D-01] Certification badges use visual icons (checkmarks in blue circles), not text list
- [D-02] All four certifications displayed: State Licensed, Fully Insured, OSHA Compliant, EPA Certified
- [D-03] Certifications placed below hero section (in Why Choose Us)
- [D-04] Certifications merged into existing Why Choose Us section (no separate section)
- [D-05] Why Choose Us section enhanced with certification badges alongside existing trust items
- [D-06] "Licensed & Fully Insured" item updated to include all four certification badges visually
- Portfolio section with 3+ categorized project samples
- Testimonials section with 2-3 client quotes
- "Request Consultation" CTA accessible within 1 click from any section on the homepage
- Phone number clickable on mobile (tel: link)
- Form captures all required fields and shows success confirmation
- All new sections responsive at 768px, 1024px, 1440px breakpoints
